# CPS Lab — Official Documentation
By: Pixlect Studios Official

## What is CPS Lab?
CPS Lab is a real-time click speed testing platform built and owned by Pixlect Studios Official. It's not just a click counter — it's a full competitive platform where players can test their speed, battle friends, chat live, and climb a global leaderboard. All in one place.

## How to Use
1. Set Your Name
Type your username in the name field at the top left. This is required to submit scores. Numbers and symbols are allowed. Rank names are protected and cannot be used by regular users.
2. Pick Your Mode
Choose one of three testing modes:

🖱️ Mouse Click — click as fast as possible. Measured in CPS (clicks per second)
⌨️ Space Bar — slam the space bar. Measured in SPS (slams per second)
🖲️ Scroll Wheel — scroll as fast as you can. Measured in pixels per second

3. Pick Your Duration
Select from: ⚡ 0.5s · 1s · 3s · 5s · 10s · 15s
4. Click the Zone to Start
The timer starts the moment you first click/slam/scroll. Keep going until time runs out.
5. See Your Results
After the test you'll see your score, a grade, your personal best, and a roast. You can then choose to submit your score to the live leaderboard or skip.

## Features
🏆 Live Leaderboard
All submitted scores are stored in real time. Anyone who opens the page sees the full leaderboard instantly. Sorted by highest score. Filter by mode. React to scores with 🔥 😂 👏.
👑 Hall of Fame
Top 10 all-time scores ever submitted. Separate from the regular board and permanent.
💬 Public Chat
A live chat room for all players. Strict word filter in place — only clean, friendly messages allowed. Rank holders appear in their rank colour with a gold ★ verification badge.
👥 Friends & Private DMs
Add friends by username, see who's online, send private messages, and challenge them to a 1v1 battle directly. Unread DM notifications appear as a badge on the friends tab.
🗂️ Group Chats
Create public or private group chats. Private groups get an invite code you can share. Public groups anyone can join from the list.
⚔️ 1v1 Battle
Challenge another player. Both sides click independently — each starts when they're ready. Live CPS shown during the battle. Winner displayed at the end. Send emoji reactions (🔥 😤 💀) to your opponent mid-battle.
📊 My Stats
Personal stats tab showing total tests, best CPS, average CPS, total clicks, a CPS history graph of your last 20 tests, and a world map showing where all players are from.
🎨 Themes
10 accent colours to personalise your interface. Saved locally so it persists across sessions. Sound effects with a toggle.
🌍 Country Flags
Your country is automatically detected from your browser timezone — no location permission needed. Shown next to your name on the leaderboard.
📤 Share Score Card
After a test, download a shareable PNG card with your score, mode, grade, country flag, and name — ready to post anywhere.
