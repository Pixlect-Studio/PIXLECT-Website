🎮 What is Minecraft?
Minecraft is a 3D "sandbox" game developed by Mojang Studios where players freely explore, gather resources, build, fight, or collaborate with others in an infinitely generated world made of blocks. 1 The game has no fixed objectives and offers highly flexible gameplay. 1

🎯 Main Game Modes
Minecraft offers several gameplay modes to suit different play styles: 2

1️⃣ Survival Mode – Gather resources, build shelters, face monsters, and manage health and hunger values 2

2️⃣ Creative Mode – Unlimited resources, flight ability, no need to worry about health or hunger, focus on building 2

3️⃣ Adventure Mode – Designed for experiencing custom maps created by others, with restrictions on breaking and placing blocks 2

4️⃣ Spectator Mode – Observation only, no interaction possible, can pass through blocks 2

🚀 How to Get Started
Choose Your Version: 3

Java Edition – Best for players who enjoy mods and custom content
Bedrock Edition – Supports cross-platform multiplayer (consoles, mobile devices)
Basic Steps: 456

Download and install from the official website or app store
Create a world and choose your game mode (Survival or Creative)
Start gathering, building, or exploring
Learn the controls – keyboard/mouse, controller, or touchscreen all work
Join multiplayer through Realms, LAN, or public servers to play with friends
Minecraft's appeal lies in its limitless creativity and freedom – you can build anything you imagine, from simple houses to complex redstone machines, or embark on adventures in vast procedurally generated worlds! ⛏️✨
