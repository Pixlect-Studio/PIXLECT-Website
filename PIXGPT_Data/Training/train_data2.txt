Projects
The studio maintains an active GitHub presence with 22 public repositories. Notable projects include:

PIXLECT-Chats — A Google Workspace‑style web application that combines email, live chat, and contact management in one platform. Users can sign up with a custom @pixlect.com address or use Google Sign‑In.

ConsoleCreator — A retro-inspired tycoon game where players build their console empire from a tiny startup to an industry giant by designing hardware, coding operating systems, researching new tech, and launching generations of consoles.

CPS-TEST — A fun way to put clicking, space bar clicking, and scrolling to the ultimate test with realtime leaderboards.

Apex-Drifter — A game built with HTML.
